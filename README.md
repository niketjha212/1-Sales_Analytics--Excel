## üéØ Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales growth, profitability, and market performance for AtliQ Hardwares, a global hardware manufacturer dealing in PCs, printers, mice, and computing accessories.

The objective was to transform raw transactional data into clear, actionable business insights that help leadership understand:
- How sales are performing against targets
- Which markets, customers, and products drive revenue
- Where profitability gaps and cost inefficiencies exist
  
The analysis was implemented using Advanced Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX, following real-world Business Intelligence practices.

---


## üìÅ Business Objective:

The core business goals addressed through this project were:

- Improve Net Sales growth while monitoring performance against predefined targets
- Enhance Gross Margin and overall profitability
- Identify high-performing and underperforming markets
- Understand customer contribution patterns
- Analyze product-level revenue concentration
- Detect cost drivers impacting margins
- Study seasonal and time-based trends in sales and profits

---

## üìÅ Dataset Overview

The dataset used for this Sales Insights project contains detailed transactional and financial information required to analyze customer performance, product profitability, and regional sales trends. The raw data was obtained from the **Codebasics Sales Insights Challenge**, a widely used real-world industry case study.

| Category | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Source** | Codebasics ‚Äì Sales Insights Data (Excel Dataset) |
| **Total Records** | ~10,000+ sales transactions |
| **Customer Data** | Customer names, zones, markets |
| **Product Data** | Product names, categories, cost & price values |
| **Sales Metrics** | Net Sales, Gross Margin, GM%, COGS |
| **Time Intelligence Data** | Fiscal Year, Fiscal Month, Fiscal Quarter |
| **Regions/Zones Covered** | 4 major zones |
| **Data Quality** | Missing values treated; data standardized & transformed |

The dataset includes multiple interconnected tables such as:  
- **Customers** (customer details & geographic mapping)  
- **Products** (product pricing, manufacturing cost, and category)  
- **Transactions** (sales amounts, gross margins, COGS)  
- **Dates Table** (fiscal months, years, and quarters)  

This structured dataset enabled comprehensive modeling and the creation of insightful performance reports using Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX.

## üß≠ Key Insights & Findings

üìà Overall Sales Performance

- Sales grew rapidly from $87.5M in 2019 to $598.9M in 2021, reflecting strong business expansion.
- Despite high growth, 2021 sales missed targets by $54.9M, leading to a 9.2% overall shortfall, highlighting execution and efficiency gaps.

## üåç Market Performance Highlights

Top Performing Markets
- India generated the highest sales at $161.3M (2021), but missed its target by $9.6M (5.9%).
- USA recorded $87.8M, falling short by $10.2M (11.7%).
- South Korea achieved $49.0M, missing the target by $4.4M (8.9%).

Underperforming Markets
- Poland showed the highest relative gap with an 18.1% shortfall.
- Canada achieved $35.1M, but missed targets by 14.5%.
- Spain recorded $12.6M, with a 14.1% deviation, indicating performance challenges.

---

## üìÇ Customer & Product Insights

- Top 20% of customers contributed nearly 50% of total sales, confirming strong customer concentration in line with the Pareto principle.
- Top 10 products accounted for ~35% of overall revenue, indicating heavy dependence on a limited set of SKUs.

---

## üìÇ Cost & Profitability Trends

- Freight and logistics costs were disproportionately high for low-quantity orders, negatively impacting margins.
- Certain markets showed strong revenue growth but weaker profitability, indicating cost optimization opportunities.

---

## üìÇ Seasonality & Time Trends

- Sales consistently peaked in Q4, revealing strong seasonal demand.
- Monthly P&L analysis helped identify high-performing and slow business periods, supporting better forecasting and planning.
